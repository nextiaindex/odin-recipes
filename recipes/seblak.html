Seblak Recipe

Description

Seblak is a Sundanese savory and spicy dish, originating from the Sundanese region in West Java, Indonesia. Made of wet krupuk (traditional Indonesian crackers) cooked with protein sources (egg, chicken, seafood or beef) in spicy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Kencur
  • Sweet soy sauce
  • Chili sauce
  • Krupuk
  • Egg
  • Any kinds of vegetables
  • Any kinds of protein sources
