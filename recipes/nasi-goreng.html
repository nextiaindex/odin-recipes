Nasi Goreng Recipe

Description

Nasi goreng is a Southeast Asian rice dish with pieces of meat and vegetables added. It can refer simply to fried pre-cooked rice, a meal including stir-fried rice in a small amount of cooking oil or margarine, typically spiced with sweet soy sauce, shallot, garlic, ground shrimp paste, chili, and accompanied by other ingredients, particularly egg, chicken, and prawns.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Ground shrimp paste
  • Chili
  • Sweet soy sauce
  • Any proteins
  • Any vegetables

Steps

  1. Mince garlic, shallot, and chili.
  2. Heat the pan until its hot.
  3. Pour oil on the pan.
  4. Saute the minced garlic, shallot, chili, ground shrimp paste, until you can smell the cooked aroma.
  5. Dunk the proteins and vegetables.
  6. Add the rice.
  7. Stir the rice so that the oil coats the rice.
  8. Add sweet soy sauce.
  9. Drizzle with sesame oil.
  10. Serve on a plate.
