Nasi Goreng Recipe

Description

Nasi goreng is a Southeast Asian rice dish with pieces of meat and vegetables added. It can refer simply to fried pre-cooked rice, a meal including stir-fried rice in a small amount of cooking oil or margarine, typically spiced with sweet soy sauce, shallot, garlic, ground shrimp paste, chili, and accompanied by other ingredients, particularly egg, chicken, and prawns.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Ground shrimp paste
  • Chili
  • Any proteins
  • Any vegetables
